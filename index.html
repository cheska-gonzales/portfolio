ABOUT ME

I am Francheska Gonzales, currently a third year college student taking up Bachelor of Arts in Multimedia Arts at De La Salle - College of Saint Benilde. Back in 2012, I gained interest in photography when I became a church photographer and when I entered college, making illustrations had caught my attention. This is a broad course which in turn gave me the room to improve and revamp my skills as well as discover my potentials as I can explore and combine various art forms and new media technologies to create quality contents. Moreover, I had the chance to express myself through art in divergent mediums so choosing to take this course offers a path to capture inspiration and creativity.
